Dan Werthimer

Chief Scientist

Dan specializes in signal processing for radio astronomy. He has been doing SETI since 1979, and he runs the SERENDIP, Optical SETI, and CASPER projects. Dan dabbles in jazz piano, and lives in Berkeley with his wife and son, William.
